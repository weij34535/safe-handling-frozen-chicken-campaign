Safe Handling of Frozen Raw Breaded Chicken

A public health campaign by Canadian Public Health Agency

Introduction

Frozen raw breaded chicken products may contain Salmonella bacteria, which can cause serious illness. Proper handling and cooking are essential to ensure safety.
